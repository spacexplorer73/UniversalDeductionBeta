Quick FYI:-


Black Hole Radius (or as it known as
"Schwarzschild Radius"
) is a parameter. Any massive entity can become a black hole if it collapses down to the Schwarzschild radius. The Schwarzschild radius just marks the radius of a sphere until the Event Horizon, past which we can get no light, no radition, no information, nothing at all!
